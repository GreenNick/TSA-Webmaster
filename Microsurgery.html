Microsurgery

One of the uses of artificial intelligence that we are exploring is during micro-surgical procedures. This has the potential of improving many medical procedures.

  • Microsurgery is intricate and precise surgery performed using miniaturized instruments.
  • Human error is very common when performing these precise operations. With AI, human error is not a factor and there will be much fewer errors.
  • According to a recent study by Johns Hopkins patient safety experts, 10 percent of all U.S. deaths are due to medical error.
  • This makes medical error the third highest cause of death in the U.S., accounting more than 250,000 deaths per year.
  • Using artificial intelligence while performing these procedures will greatly decrease these numbers.
  • Being able to learn from its mistakes to become virtually perfect is an advantage that artificial intelligence can provide.
  • Another advantage that artificial intelligence can provide while being used as an alternative to real doctors is that the doctors will not need to be paid, so, overtime, using AI will become cheaper than the alternative.
