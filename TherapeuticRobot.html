Therapeutic Robots

One way to implement artificial intelligence is to use it in therapeutic settings.

  • Many times during therapeutic sessions, the patient cannot connect with the therapist.
  • This decreases the amount of help that the patient can actually receive, and might discourage the patient from returning back to therapy.
  • Using robots with artificial intelligence disguised as a therapeutic animal or doll can be used as a new form of therapy.
  • Patients can specify which body they want their “doll” to have, and will be able to customize it to their preference.
  • This will help patients form a close connection with it and be able to calm down and relieve the tension and aggression they have.
  • Robots disguised as an object the patient likes. For example, a cat, a dog, or teddy bear.
  • Serves as a comforter for those in pain as well as Alzheimer’s patients
  • Allows people to stay within their households with less medical assistance
  • Emergency alert button could be attached
  • Tells when the patient needs to take a medication
  • Requires less human vigilance
